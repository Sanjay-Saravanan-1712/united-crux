Recipe

Here's the recipe for today's dish:

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving 1/4 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until the garlic is golden and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes. Be careful not to let the garlic burn.
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the garlic and oil. Toss to coat the spaghetti evenly with the garlic-infused oil. If the spaghetti seems too dry, add a splash of the reserved pasta water.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Toss again to combine.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Ingredients

Here's a list of ingredients you'll need:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
